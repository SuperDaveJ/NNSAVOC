Health Clinic

The NRC provides an on-site health clinic at One White Flint North, on the 2nd Floor.  It is staffed with nurses and a doctor.  Services provided at the health clinic are free and include:

  • Emergency diagnosis and treatment
    • For injuries that occur on NRC Headquarters property
  • Occupational injury/illness examination
  • Walk-in evaluation and limited treatment for minor illnesses
  • Various screening programs
    • Diabetes
    • Glaucoma/vision testing
    • High blood pressure testing
    • Allergy shots (if prescribed by their personal physician)
  • Physicals
  • Ergonomic assessments
