Office of Human Resources

The Office of Human Resources provides overall leadership and management for agency human capital planning and for human resources planning, policy, and program development.Â  Its functions include:

  • Develop and implement the HR strategic plan, performance plan, and operating plan
  • Assist and advise NRC management in the planning and implementation of human capital goals
  • Establish accountability for achievement of human capital goals
  • Monitor performance and provide feedback
  • Plan and implement NRC policies, programs, and services to provide for:
    • Employment services and operations
    • Training
    • Employee and labor relations
    • Organizational development
    • Workforce information and analysis
  • Administer and manage NRC work-life services and occupational safety and health programs, including:
    • Oversight of the workers' compensation and employee assistance programs
    • Headquarters child care facility
    • Headquarters health unit
    • Headquarters fitness center
  • Advise and support the planning, development, implementation, oversight, and evaluation of HR information systems
  • Conduct formulation, justification, and execution activities for the human capital budget and for human resources office budget.
