Emergency Operations Quick Fact

The U.S. Government’s Nuclear Incident Response Teams reside within NNSA’s Office of Emergency Operations.  These teams maintain the Nation’s capability to respond to a nuclear or radiological incident or emergency anywhere in the world.  They are the Nation’s last line of defense. 

Nuclear Incident Response Teams are maintained in a state of readiness to meet the requirement for their deployment within hours of notification to search for, locate, and manage a nuclear or radiological incident, anywhere in the world.  No other Federal department or private institution has a similar capability.  These teams support and work with the Federal Bureau of Investigations, Department of Defense, Department of Homeland Security, and the Nuclear Regulatory Commission.
