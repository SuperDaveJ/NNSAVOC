Cafeterias are located in both the Forrestal and Germantown buildings. Hours of operation for the Forrestal Cafeteria are: 6:45 am to 3:00 pm, Monday through Friday. For the Germantown cafeteria, operating hours are 7:00 am to 2:30 pm, Monday through Friday.

Standard breakfast and lunch foods are made at specialty stations, with hot and cold self-serve buffets providing a wide range of foods priced by weight.
