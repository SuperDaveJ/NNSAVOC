Flexible Spending Accounts

Health Care Flexible Spending Accounts (HCFSA) and Dependent Care Flexible Spending Accounts (DCFSA) reimburse for out of pocket expenses. 

Enrollment is completed online, and must be finished within 60 days of your first day on the job.  Your premiums are withheld on a pre-tax basis, and are subject to IRS-issued household limits.  No new FSA accounts are permitted after October 1st of a given year.  However, open season is from mid-November to mid-December.  Changes made during open season take effect on the next January 1st.  You must elect FSA yearly; as election does not carryover from year to year.

For more information, please visit: http://opm.gov/insure/.
