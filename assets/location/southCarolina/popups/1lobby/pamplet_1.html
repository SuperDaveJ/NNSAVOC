Fitness Center

In H-Area, Federal employees have access to a small, limited fitness room. This fitness location is maintained and operated by the Site Security contractor. Federal employees are allowed to utilize this facility if safety orientation is completed.

In F-Area, MOX Services maintains and operates a fitness facility that is available to all NNSA Federal staff. Federal staff are allowed to utilize this facility if a safety orientation is completed. This facility includes 8 treadmills, 4 elliptical, 3 bikes, free weights, and circuit training.
