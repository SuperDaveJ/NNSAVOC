Dental and Vision

The Federal Employee Dental and Vision Insurance Program (FEDVIP) is available to eligible employees and their eligible family members.  If you are eligible for FEHB, then you’re eligible for FEDVIP.  You do not need to be enrolled in an FEHB plan to participate in FEDVIP. Premiums are withheld on a pretax basis.

Enrollment is completed online and should be finished within 60 days of your first day on the job.  You have three choices in your enrollment:

  • Self-only
  • Self plus one
  • Self and family

Once you have enrolled, your enrollment continues automatically from year to year.  Changes to coverage can be made during the annual open season (November/December). In addition, changes can be made in the event of a valid qualifying life event.  You may cancel only during open season.

You may have questions about the specifics of each plan (limits, co-pays, deductible, network locations, out of network costs, etc.).  Because there are several plans available to you, and the coverage varies from one plan to another, it is best to review each plan’s details carefully.  Plan information can be found at: http://opm.gov/insure/.
