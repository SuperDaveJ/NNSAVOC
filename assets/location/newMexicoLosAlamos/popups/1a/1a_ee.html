Office of Administration

The Office of Administration (ADM) provides centralized services in the areas of:

  • Contracts
  • Facilities and security
  • Property management
  • Administrative services

These administrative services include:

  • Rulemaking
  • Agency directives support
  • Transportation
  • Parking
  • Translations
  • Audiovisual
  • Food services
  • Mail distribution
  • Labor services
  • Furniture and supplies
