The Energy Federal Credit Union

The Energy Federal Credit Union (EFCU) offers the best available products and services to meet your savings, investment, and loan requirements. From a knowledgeable staff, to convenient electronic services and ATM locations, the EFCU is available to you 24 hours a day.

There are EFCU branches at the Forrestal and Germantown buildings, the Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) headquarters in Bethesda, MD, and the Administrative Office in Rockville, MD.
