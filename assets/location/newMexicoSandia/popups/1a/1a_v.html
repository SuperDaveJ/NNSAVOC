Office of Small Business and Civil Rights

The responsibilities of the Office of Small Business and Civil Rights (SBCR) include:

  • Facilitates equal employment opportunity for all NRC employees and applicants for employment through an on-going affirmative employment and diversity management process.
  • Provides for prompt, fair and impartial processing of discrimination complaints filed under applicable Civil Rights statutes, and eliminates or modifies procedures or practices that give rise to valid complaints.
  • Ensures that small, disadvantaged, 8(a), women-owned, HUBZone, and service-disabled veteran-owned small businesses have full and fair opportunity to participate in NRC procurement activities.
  • Implements White House education initiatives and provisions of the Energy Policy Act of 2005, to ensure that Minority Serving Institutions (MSIs) such as Historically Black Colleges and Universities, Hispanic Serving Institutions and Tribal Colleges and Universities, and students and faculty of MSIs have meaningful and equal access to NRC-conducted and financially assisted programs and activities.
