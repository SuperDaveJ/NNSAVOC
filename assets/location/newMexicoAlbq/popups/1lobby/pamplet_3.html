Occupational Health Clinic

The NNSA Health Clinic, managed by the Office of Secure Transportation (OST), is located in Building 383, 1st floor, North end. The clinic provides health services for Federal OST, NNSA Albuquerque Complex, Site Offices, and HQ employees located in or visiting Albuquerque.

The clinic also conducts Human Reliability Program medical examinations, as well as walk-in health care and preventative occupational health services.

Contact the Health Clinic at (505) 845-6677 or (505) 845-6682 for further information.
