Deputy Executive Director for Corporate Management

The Deputy Executive Director for Corporate Management executes OEDO responsibility for corporate services which include:

  • Information technology (IT) infrastructure (network, help desk, telephones, video-teleconferencing), applications development (technology assessment), and project management (system development life cycle management).
  • Information management services (technical library, internal/external Web site, electronic document and records management system, Freedom of Information Act).
  • Information systems security oversight (certification and accreditation of IT systems, policy development, training, cyber incident response/situational awareness).
  • Agency space planning and facilities management, personnel and facility security, rulemaking, acquisition management, and centralized administrative services (parking, mail, printing, graphics, audio/visual services, editing, furniture and supplies, event support).
  • In addition, the Director represents the NRC on the Federal Chief Information Officer Council, and carries out the day-to-day supervision, guidance, and direction of the Office of Information Services, Computer Security Office and Office of Administration.
