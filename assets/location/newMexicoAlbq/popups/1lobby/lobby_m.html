Parking

A large, free outdoor parking lot is available and spans the entire north side of the Albuquerque Complex, with a smaller parking lot on the south side. There are designated areas/spots for persons with disabilities. Motorcycle, assigned, and credit union customer parking spaces are also available.

Bicycle racks are located near the Albuquerque Complex entrances.

Employees and visitors must show identification to enter Kirtland Air Force Base to enter into the Albuquerque Complex.
