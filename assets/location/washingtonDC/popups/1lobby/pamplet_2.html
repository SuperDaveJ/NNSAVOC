Fitness Center

The DOE is committed to promoting health and fitness among Federal employees, contractors, and retirees. DOE maintains on-site fitness centers at both the Forrestal and Germantown locations. In these centers, you will find a complete selection of group classes and equipment, including bikes, treadmills, and weights.

The Forrestal Occupational Health Organization (FOHO) is located in room GM-159 and is open Monday and Friday from 6:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m. and Tuesday through Thursday from 6:00 a.m. to 7:30 p.m.

The Germantown Occupational Health Organization (GOHO) is located in room S-140 and is open Monday through Friday from 6:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m.
