You may be wondering:  What do I need to do before my first day at the NNSA?  That’s a good question!  First, become familiar with everything on this site.  In most cases, if you want more information, links are available to help you get specifics.

Before arriving at NNSA you will be contacted by the Human Resources office to arrange your acceptance process.  The information from HR will include your start date as well as what you will need to complete to obtain your security clearance, if one is required.

Review the resources in this room to acquaint yourself with what to expect on your first day.
