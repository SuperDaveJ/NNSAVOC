Life Insurance Information

All NNSA employees automatically receive basic Federal Employee Group Life Insurance (FEGLI).  Through payroll deduction, employees may elect to increase the basic coverage and/or add family coverage.  After the first day on the job, they have 31 days to elect additional coverage.  After this initial election, they cannot increase coverage, but they may decrease it at any time.  If coverage is waived by the end of the first pay period, no premiums will be deducted.

Although open enrollment periods do occur, they are rare.  If an employee decides to waive the insurance, but changes his mind later, he must wait one year and provide medical information before applying.  Certain specified coverage may be added within 60 days of a qualifying life event (marriage, divorce, death of a spouse, or birth or adoption of a child).

For more information, please visit: http://opm.gov/insure/life/.
