Other Services

The Forrestal Building offers a barbershop (room GD-020), which requires appointments and is open Monday through Thursday from 9:15 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. Shoe shine services are available in the lower lobby.

The Germantown Building’s barbershop is located in room E-001. It is open Monday through Thursday from 8:30 a.m. through 3:30 p.m. and Friday from 9:30 a.m. through 2:30 p.m. Various retail vendors are scheduled daily at both the Forrestal and Germantown locations. A schedule of vendors is published in the Energy Recreation and Welfare Association’s monthly newsletter.
