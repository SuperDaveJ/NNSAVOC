Groups, Organizations, and Activities Avatar

Something’s always going on at the NNSA, and not just work. What is your favorite hobby? What do you like to do in your spare time? Would you like to get together with people who have similar interests? There are numerous groups and organizations that have formed for that very purpose.

The Energy Recreation and Welfare Association (ERWA) has club and activity contacts in their monthly newsletter. DOE’s Employee Assistance Program also has contact information for a variety of educational and wellness programs.
