Parking

Ample outdoor parking is available at the North Las Vegas Complex. There are some designated areas/slots for persons with disabilities, motorcycle, and Van Pool and government assigned parking. Overflow parking is also available within walking distance.

Employees and visitors must show identification in order to enter the North Las Vegas Complex and the Nevada National Security Site to park at, or enter into the NNSA Nevada Site Office. Badging must be arranged prior to the visit and can be picked up North Las Vegas Office or NNSS Badge Office.
