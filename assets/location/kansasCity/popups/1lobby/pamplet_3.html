Occupational Health Clinic

The DOE provides an on-site health clinic at both the Forrestal and Germantown locations. Services provided at the health clinic are free to Federal employees and include:

  • Walk-in first-aid services for non-life threatening injuries and illnesses
  • Health seminars and blood drives
  • Lactation services
  • Physician directed services, such as allergy injections and blood pressure monitoring
  • Influenza vaccinations
  • First-response emergency treatment to any employee, contractor, or visitor needing immediate care

The Forrestal clinic is located in room GG-208. Hours of operation are 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Monday through Thursday. It is closed on Friday except for emergencies.
