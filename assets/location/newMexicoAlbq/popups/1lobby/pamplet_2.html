Fitness/Wellness Program

The Wellness Program offers health and wellness services provided as a benefit to DOE/NNSA federal employees. The Wellness Program is housed in a fully equipped fitness center. The Wellness Program is a self-service facility open from 6:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m., Monday through Friday. The NNSA Albuquerque Complex is located on Kirtland Air Force Base which has many fitness activities going on year round that are also available to DOE/NNSA employees.
