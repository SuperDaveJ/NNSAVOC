Eligibility:

Exercise classes require authorized access for participants to use the Wellness Center exercise facility. Exercise classes are restricted to employees of Los Alamos Security, LLC (LANL), DOE direct employees, Pro-Force SOC-LA, LACFD, and specified contractor/affiliates.

Exercise Class Policies:

Please read the Exercise Class Policies prior to taking a class. The class policies explain the class requirements, class levels, and cancellations.

Exercise classes are offered free of charge to all eligible Wellness Center customers, no registration required. Due to room capacity requirements, there is a strict 30-student maximum per class unless otherwise noted in the brochure. Classes fill on a first-come, first-served basis and participants will secure their space by signing the class roster each time they come in to attend a class.
