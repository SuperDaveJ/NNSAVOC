Fitness Center

The NRC maintains a state-of-the-art fitness center in the headquarters building complex at Two White Flint North, on the 1st floor.  You will find a complete selection of equipment, including bikes, treadmills, and weights.  In addition, nutritional and health programs are sponsored by the Fitness Center during lunch periods.  A one-time initial fee of $25 gets you started.  After that, biweekly payroll deductions keep you on your workout schedule.
