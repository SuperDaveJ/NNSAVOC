The Savannah River Federal Employees (SRFE) is an association of Federal employees and Support Service Contractors (SSC) at the Savannah River Site (SRS) united for the purposes of providing recreational, educational, charitable, social and athletic activities through joint endeavors by its members. All Feds/SSCs associated with SRS are encouraged to join SRFE, and the annual dues are only $10.00. Upcoming Events are posted on the SRFE website, as well as our Discount program and various Services.
