Long-term Care

Long-term care (LTC) provides reimbursement for services such as home care, assisted living, and nursing home care. If you are eligible for FEHB, then you are eligible for LTC. You do not have to be enrolled in an FEHB plan to participate in LTC. You may also enroll eligible family members. The enrollment process must be completed online within 60 days of your appointment. Your LTC benefits will be effective on the 1st day of the month after your approval.

For more information, please visit: http://opm.gov/insure/.
