Parking

Forrestal Building

Monthly permit parking is only available to carpools and vanpools, employees who work unusual hours, and employees with disabilities. There is a parking committee that reviews and approves applications for parking permits.

Temporary permits are sold in the parking booth located in the garage. Employees are allowed up to 5 one-day permits per month.

Limited visitor parking spaces are available on a first-come-first-served basis by contacting the Forrestal parking garage.

There is space designated for bicycle parking in the Forrestal garage.

Same day parking permit purchase is not available; permits must be purchased in advance of the day the employee wishes to park. Employees have until 1PM the day before their intended parking day to purchase a permit from the garage.

Germantown Building

Permits are not required to park in Germantown.  However, if you need to leave your car overnight, you will require approval.  Employees and visitors must show identification in order to enter the complex.

Germantown also has designated bicycle parking areas.
