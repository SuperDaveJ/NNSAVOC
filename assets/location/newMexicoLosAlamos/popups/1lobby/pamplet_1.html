Fitness Center

The Los Alamos National Laboratory provides a comprehensive program of on-site fitness classes and other services which is open to all LANL and onsite resident Federal employees. These classes and services help you maintain a healthy lifestyle within the time constraints of a busy work schedule.

Individual assessment and consultants are available with Wellness Center staff in the areas of fitness, nutrition, post rehabilitation exercised as follows:

  • Equipment Orientation-Overview of equipment and basic exercise program setup.
  • Fitness Consultation-Meet with a health/fitness specialist to discuss fitness goals, barriers/motivation to exercise, etc.
  • Fitness Testing-Assesses your personal fitness level and generates a fitness report to identify areas of weakness.

Appointments:

To set up a time to meet with a Health/Fitness Specialist, call 505-667-6528.

Eligibility:

Fitness assessments and consultants are available by appointment to current clients of the Wellness Center, including Los Alamos National Security (LANS), Department of Energy (DOE), SOC-LA, COMPA and Los Alamos County Firefighters (LACFD). Unfortunately, fitness assessments and consultations are not available to contractors, spouses, and retirees, but if they would like information concerning specific health and fitness issues, Wellness Center staff can make appointments to discuss those matters.
