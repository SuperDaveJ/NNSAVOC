The main on-site cafeteria located at the Los Alamos National Laboratory is in the Otowi Building at TA-3 Building 261 on the 3rd floor. It offers full service breakfast and lunch. The cafeteria is within walking or biking distance from each of the on-site facilities. Hours of operation are:

Monday thru Friday, 6:30 a.m. to 10:00 a.m. for breakfast and 11:00 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. for lunch.

Catering is offered through the Otowi Cafeteria, including a variety of choices from classic favorites to contemporary cuisine and healthy dining options. More than great food, they deliver great service. For more information contact the catering manager or coordinator.
