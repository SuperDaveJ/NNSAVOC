Welcome to the NRC Virtual Orientation Center (VOC).  Please take a moment to enter your name, and some basic information, then we’ll get started.

Congratulations on your new employment with the NRC, one of the best places to work in the Federal Government! You probably have a number of questions as you begin your journey.  Let’s start with this question: How can you learn more about the NRC before you arrive?  That is exactly what the VOC is designed to do.

You will find that the VOC has three floors, and each floor has one or more rooms.  The rooms, hallways, lobby, and elevator all contain information for you.  You may access this information in any order, and at any time you wish. It is not necessary to complete your tour of the Orientation Center in one sitting.  If you need to leave, simply log off and come back later.  Be sure to visit each floor, each room, and explore every available interaction. Each interaction will give you the essentials about the topic.  In many cases you will find links to sites where you can get more information.

Use the arrow keys to move forward, turn around, or turn to the left or right.  As your avatar turns, you will see the room move, just as you do when you move your head, or turn your body in a non-virtual environment.  Use the elevator to move from one floor to another.  Again, it is not necessary to move through the VOC in order.  You will find a directory by the elevator on each floor.  As you look around the VOC, use it to help you decide where you want to go next.

If you have any questions, the Automated Assistant can be accessed by clicking on the icon in the lower left-hand corner.

That should do it for now.  Enjoy looking around, and welcome to the NRC!
