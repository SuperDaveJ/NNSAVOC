Fitness Center

SFO staff may also participate in the DOE/NNSA Albuquerque Complex Wellness Program which offers employees a wide range of cost-free fitness classes. You can sign up for any of these, and others, by contacting the Wellness Center staff.

  • Step: Step is a great class for those new to step as well as more experience participants. It incorporates fun combinations while keeping your cardiovascular intensity consistent throughout the class.
  • Fit Ball Toning: The Fit Ball workout is a toning class which utilizes the latest in fitness equipment, the "fitness ball". You will improve core strength and balance while toning your body. The hottest new fitness workout!
  • Strengthening and Toning: Toning classes are designed to increase muscular strength and tone. Body weight, free weights, fit balls and dynabands are used to increase resistance in exercises. Abs and stretching conclude each session. Great for beginners!
  • Yoga/Pilates: Are you looking for a fitness routine with a little more stretching and a little less bouncing? This is the workout for you! Focus is on core strengthening and balance.
