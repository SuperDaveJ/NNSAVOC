Hi!  Carol asked me to stop by and tell you about the National Treasury Employees Union, or NTEU.  The NTEU is widely known as a smart, tough organization.  It’s well-respected for its knowledge of Federal employee issues as well as its determination to work with Federal agencies, Congress, and the courts to protect, promote, and expand the rights of those it represents.  

For 70 years, the NTEU has been driven by the principle that every Federal employee should be treated with dignity and respect.  NTEU represents over 150,000 bargaining unit employees in 31 Federal agencies and departments. 

NTEU members are represented by an experienced and professional staff in Washington, D.C., seven field offices across the nation, and highly trained, dedicated, local leaders in their workplaces.
