There is one cafeteria located at the North Las Vegas Complex of the Nevada Site Office and two at the Nevada National Security Site (NNSS) that offer full service breakfast and lunch. The North Las Vegas cafeteria is located at the Nevada Support Facility and is within walking distance from all North Las Vegas facilities.

Hours of operation are:

  • NSF - Espresso King Café: 6:30 a.m. – 9:30 a.m. and 11:00 a.m. – 1:30 p.m.
  • NNSS Main Cafeteria: 5:30 a.m. – 7:30 a.m. and 11:00 a.m. – 1:00 p.m.
  • NNSS Forward Area Snack Bar: 11:00 a.m. – 1:00 p.m.
