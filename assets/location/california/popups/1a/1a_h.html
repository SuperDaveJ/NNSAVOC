Office of Congressional Affairs

The mission of the Office of Congressional Affairs (OCA) is to:

  • Provide the Chairman, members of the Commission, and all NRC employees with timely and reliable information as it pertains to legislative matters affecting the NRC in the United States Congress.
  • Advise and ensure proper protocol with regards to visits by members of the Commission and others to the Congress and other agencies.
  • Ensure correct and timely information is distributed to other Federal agencies and external organizations.
