Child Development Center

Georgetown Hill Early School is the name of the NRC Child Development Center.  It is located on the P-1 level of the Two White Flint North headquarters complex.  Children as young as 6 weeks and as old as 6 years may be enrolled here.  Class hours are 6:45 am to 6:00 pm.

The school program develops basic readiness skills, and encourages the children to challenge and test themselves and experience the joys of learning in a whole-child environment.  The Center includes all-day Kindergarten.

If you have elected the Flexible Spending Account (FSA) option as a benefit, you may use that account for some or all of your Child Development Center expenses.
