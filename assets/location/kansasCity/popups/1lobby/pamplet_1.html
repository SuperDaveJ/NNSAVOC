Fitness Facility

The Kansas City National Security Campus (NSC) provides a 1,700 sf state of the art fitness facility with on-site fitness classes open to all NSC employees.  

Classes are taught by certified instructors providing fun and diverse exercise options, including boot camps, yoga, Tabata, and Zumba.

Cardiovascular equipment at the facility includes treadmills, elliptical trainers, stationary bicycles and more. The facility also has a weight training area complete with free weights and machines used to perform several different exercises along with body bars, exercise balls, and resistance tubing.  Showers, lockers, and towels are also provided.

In addition, the staff offers complimentary health assessments, personal program designs and nutritional assessments. Every four to six weeks a wellness program is offered. The facility is open to all NSC employees at no cost.
