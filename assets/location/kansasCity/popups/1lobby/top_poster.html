Family Friendly Leave

Family friendly leave is one reason the NNSA is a great place to work in the Federal Government.  The term “family friendly leave” encompasses many situations.  It includes the opportunity to be present for the birth or adoption of a child, provide health care for a family member, or attend the funeral of a family member.  More specific information is available at the leave kiosk in the second floor elevator bay.

On-Site Amenities

Although they differ somewhat from site to site, the NNSA has a number of on-site amenities, including:

      • A Fitness Center
      • A Health and Wellness Center
      • Cafeterias and snack bar
      • Parking

You will find more information on each of these as you look around the first floor lobby.
