Medical

As a Federal employee you are eligible to elect Federal Employee Health Benefit (FEHB) coverage, unless your position is excluded by law or regulation.  You must return all enrollment forms to HR within 60 calendar days of your first day of employment.  After the forms have been received, and a full pay period has passed, your medical benefits will be effective.  Your medical benefits are deducted using pre-tax dollars.

What about changing your benefits?  They may be changed during open season, which is mid-November to mid-December each year.

You may have questions about plans and coverage (limits, co-pays, deductibles, network locations, out of network costs, etc.).  There are several plans available to you, and you can read about each plan and their specific details at http://opm.gov/insure/.
