Occupational Health Clinic

SFO employees may obtain services offered at the NNSA Albuquerque Complex Health Clinic. The Health Clinic is managed by the Office of Secure Transportation (OST) and is located in Building 20383, 1st floor north end.

The NNSA Health Clinic provides health services for Federal OST, NNSA Albuquerque Complex, Field Offices and HQ employees located in Albuquerque and visiting Albuquerque.

The NNSA Health Clinic conducts the Human Reliability Program medical examinations, as well as walk-in health care and preventative occupational health services.

Contact the Health Unit at (505) 845-6677 or (505) 845-6682 for further information.
