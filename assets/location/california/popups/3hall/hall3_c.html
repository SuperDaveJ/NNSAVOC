Quick Fact – Counterterrorism Agreements

The NNSA Office of Counterterrorism leads an interagency effort to tighten security for weapons-usable nuclear materials worldwide, including:

  • Bilateral, interagency interactions with Russia, France, the United Kingdom, Japan, and Belgium on physical protection, threat
    assessments, training and evaluation, exercises, and research and development
  • Bilateral MOUs strengthen indigenous counterterrorism capabilities and facilitate technical exchanges
  • Support to the Global Initiative to Combat Nuclear Terrorism, IAEA, World Institute for Nuclear Security (WINS), and other international efforts
