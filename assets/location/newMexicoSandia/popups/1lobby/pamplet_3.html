Kirtland Air Force Bas

Employees and visitors must show identification in order to enter the Kirtland Air Force Base. Badging must be arranged prior to the visit and can be picked up along with a site map at the Innovation Parkway Office Corridor.

All visitors must have a driver's/operator's license, vehicle insurance and registration paperwork to receive a visitor's pass to access the base. These passes are only good for three days. Wyoming, Gibson, and Truman Gates offer these the passes.

Contractors performing work on the base must have a appropriate driver's/operator's license vehicle insurance and registration paperwork to receive a pass to access the base Contractors must use the Truman Gate to receive a base. Each Contractor must be sponsored by an employee of the DOE or NNSA.
