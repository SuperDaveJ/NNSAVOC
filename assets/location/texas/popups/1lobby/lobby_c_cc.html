There are two on-site cafeterias that offer full service breakfast and lunch. The cafeterias are within walking or biking distance from across the Site. Hours of operation are from 6:00 a.m. to 8:00 a.m. for breakfast and 11:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. for lunch (except for Friday when the cafeterias close at 12:30 p.m.)
