Employee Self Service Information

The Employee Self Service (ESS) system enables DOE employees to view their own payroll, personal and training information and update certain information on the Internet. Currently, employees are able to update the following information online through ESS:

  • Home address
  • Emergency contacts
  • Education
  • License and certification information
  • Voluntary allotments
  • Federal and state tax withholdings
  • Direct deposit of the paycheck
  • Enrollment information for the Thrift Savings Plan (TSP)

You can also use ESS to complete and submit your automated Individual Development Plan.
