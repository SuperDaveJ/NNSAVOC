Occupational Health Clinic

NPO employees have access to the following services through the Pantex Medical Department:

  • Walk-in first-aid services for non-life threatening injuries
  • Blood Drives
  • Influenza Vaccinations
  • First-response emergency treatment to any employee, contractor, or visitor needing immediate care
  • Mandatory, work-related physicals and exams (determined by position held)
  • Prescription Safety Glasses (vendor provided)
  • Work boots/shoes
  • Bio-assays
  • Ergonomic evaluations of work stations
