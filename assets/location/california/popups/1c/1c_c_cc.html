Energy Recreation and Welfare Association Avatar

The DOE Energy Recreation and Welfare Association (ERWA) is a non-profit organization created to promote and sponsor those cultural, educational, recreational, social, welfare, and related membership activities that accrue benefits to improve employee morale.

The shops offer discount tickets, used books, jigsaw puzzles, and CDs for sale; pamphlets and brochures with discount offers from hotels, entertainment, and other activities near vacation areas. The ERWA Shops are a source of information for all ERWA activities.

ERWA is located in Forrestal Building, room GE-139, and can be contacted at ERWAFORS@hq.doe.gov. The shop is staffed by volunteers and open Thursdays from 11:30 am to 1:30 pm. 

The Germantown ERWA shop is located in room E-101 and is open Thursday and Friday from 11:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. Or you can visit the website by going to www.recgov.org and selecting Energy.
