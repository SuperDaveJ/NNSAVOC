Groups, Organizations, and Activities

In addition to the outstanding career opportunities at the NNSA, you will find a number of events, extracurricular activities, groups, and organizations.  More information on these topics is available in Room 1C.

Travel

If you like to travel, there are many opportunities at the NNSA.  The NNSA has multiple offices in various locations.  In some cases, you may be needed to travel internationally to represent the NNSA. More information about travel is available in the second floor elevator bay.

Transportation Benefits

Do you use public transportation to get to work?  If so, you may be eligible for a transportation subsidy each month.  Both the Forrestal and Germantown locations are located near bus stops, and the Forrestal office is located near multiple metro lines and the VRE.  Take a look at the Transportations booth in Room 1C for more information.
