Greeting

Hello. I’m Tom D’Agostino, Under Secretary for Nuclear Security and the Administrator of the National Nuclear Security Administration.

I want to welcome you to our team. Our mission is to ensure the safety, security, and the effectiveness of our nation’s nuclear stockpile; to prevent the proliferation of nuclear weapons of mass destruction; to keep terrorists from acquiring nuclear and radiological materials; and to support our nuclear-powered Navy.

We also respond to nuclear and radiological emergencies in the United States and abroad. In addition, NNSA Federal agents provide safe and secure transportation of nuclear weapons and other special nuclear materials.

The work at NNSA will challenge you. You’ll be engaged in some of the most important national security issues our country faces. You will work with some of the brightest professionals in the world. You’ll routinely tackle highly complex, technical, and scientific problems.

Here at NNSA, we have a collegial, collaborative, and inclusive work environment that gives employees the opportunity to grow professionally. We reward employees through our Pay for Performance system, and we promote a work-life balance all while protecting national security.

By choosing a job at NNSA, you will have the satisfaction of serving your country and the knowledge that you are contributing to the nation’s security.

Welcome to the NNSA, and I hope you enjoy exploring the Virtual Orientation Center.
