Quick Fact – Recycling Program

Recycling information available on the Pollution Prevention Program (internal website). Recycling information is listed for 18 different areas.

The Aluminum Beverage Can Program (ABC) within Pollution Prevention allows employees to donate cans, then nominate their favorite charities to receive a $200 donation from the recycled cans - if approved by the ABC committee.
