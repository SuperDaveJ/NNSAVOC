Fitness Center

These classes and services help you maintain a healthy lifestyle within the time constraints of a busy work schedule.

Fitstop is an on-site workout facility offering the latest in cardiovascular equipment including cybex treadmills, elliptical trainers, stationary bicycles, the cybex glider and more. Fitstop also has a weight training area complete with free weights, smith press, bench press, a cybex multi-station used to perform several different exercises along with body bars, exercise balls, and resistance tubing.

NOTE: LLESA Membership registration is required along with a membership fee to use the services offered.
