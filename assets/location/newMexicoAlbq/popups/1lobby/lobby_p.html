Off-site Dining

Numerous and varied food options exist within walking distance of each location. Whatever your dining need, from easy and quick, to sit-down and relax, chances are it’s only a short walk away. Within walking distance, the Bowling Center has a large café called the Sparetime Grill. It offers a large variety of items such as hamburgers, hotdogs, pizza, green chile stew, and many daily specials.

Also on base is the Bogey Bar and Grill located at the Tijeras Arroyo Golf Course. You will need a vehicle, however, to travel the 3 or 4 miles to the golf course.
