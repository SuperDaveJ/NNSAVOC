Fitness Center

Pantex provides an on-site workout facility offering the latest in cardiovascular equipment including Cybex treadmills, elliptical trainers, stationary bicycles, the Cybex glider and more. The facility also has a weight training area complete with free weights, smith press, bench press, a Cybex multi-station used to perform several different exercises along with body bars, exercise balls, and resistance tubing.

NOTE: Personnel must successfully complete the required medical screening process to use the fitness facility and the adjacent outdoor running track. Interested employees should contact Pantex Medical or the Security Training Fitness Specialists for details.
