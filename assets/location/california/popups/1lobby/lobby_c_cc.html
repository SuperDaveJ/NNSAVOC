There are two on-site cafeterias located at the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory that offer full service breakfast and lunch. The cafeterias are within walking or biking distance from each of its on-site facilities. Hours of operation are:

  • Central Café: 6:30 a.m. to 9:30 a.m. for breakfast and 11:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. for lunch.
  • West Café: 6:30 a.m. to 9:30 a.m. for breakfast and 11:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. for lunch.
